Help Center

Find answers to common questions about our community event portal.

Getting Started

How do I create an account?

Click on the "Register" button on the home page and fill in your details. You'll receive a confirmation email to verify your account.

How do I log in?

Use the email address and password you created during registration to log in to the portal.

How do I recover my password?

Click on "Forgot your password?" on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your email.

Event Registration

How do I register for an event?

Navigate to the "Events" page, find an event you're interested in, and click "Register Now". Fill in your details and confirm your registration.

Can I register for multiple events?

Yes! You can register for as many events as you'd like. All your registrations will appear on your dashboard.

How do I cancel my event registration?

Go to your Dashboard, find the event you want to cancel, and click "Cancel Registration". You'll receive a confirmation email.

What is the cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 7 days before the event are eligible for a full refund. Cancellations made within 7 days may have reduced refunds.

Profile Management

How do I update my profile?

Click on your profile icon in the dashboard and select "Edit Profile" to update your personal information.

Can I change my email address?

Yes, you can change your email address in your profile settings. A verification email will be sent to confirm the change.

How do I delete my account?

Visit your profile settings and select "Delete Account". Please note that this action is permanent.

Feedback & Reviews

How do I submit feedback for an event?

After attending an event, go to your dashboard and select the event to submit your feedback and rating.

Can I leave a review for the portal?

Yes! Visit the Feedback page to share your experience using our community event portal.

Technical Issues

The website isn't loading properly. What should I do?

Try clearing your browser cache and cookies, then reload the page. If the issue persists, try a different browser.

I'm not receiving confirmation emails.

Check your spam or junk mail folder. Make sure to add info@communityeventportal.com to your contacts.

Still Need Help?

Can't find the answer you're looking for? Contact our support team and we'll be happy to assist you!
